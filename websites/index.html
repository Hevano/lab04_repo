Problem

Online collaborative brainstorming tools are limited and outdated compared to the real-life brainstorming techniques we use today.

 

 

Resource and Limitations

  • Old Fashion
  • Lack Structure
  • Unspecific
  • Blank Canvas
  • Only help described ideas

 

 

Solution

Rapid ideation tool and Dot-voting

  • Project leader comes up with a theme
  • Allocate a short amount of time for people to write whatever comes to mind
  • App records all answers into a database and counts “like responses”
  • Once the first stage of brainstorming is finished, users can vote on their favourite ideas.
  • The ideas are then publicly ranked
